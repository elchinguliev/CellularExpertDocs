## Notes

- **⚠️ flag** = more than 15% of that document's PDF pages with images could not be confidently text-matched to a location in the markdown, so a small number of images were placed near the closest earlier confirmed anchor instead of their exact page. Worth a manual spot-check.
- **CE Pro Training docs** (00-installation through 10-rl-prediction) are the main weak spot: their markdown was rewritten/summarized rather than a verbatim PDF-to-text conversion, so page text often doesn't match closely enough to auto-place images with full confidence.
- **Logos/icons removed**: any image that is byte-identical to another image appearing 3+ times within the same PDF, OR appearing in 8+ different PDFs across the whole set of 31 manuals, is treated as decorative (the Cellular Expert banner, generic bullet/checkbox icons, etc.) and is not inserted into the docs. A copy of each is kept once under each document's `repeated/` subfolder for reference, not shown inline.
- Everything else — every unique screenshot and icon that actually appears in the PDF content — is kept and placed, including images shared between closely related guides (e.g. the two CE Express admin guides), which are real content, not logos.
